Technology

Band drilling has many advantages over traditional seed drills, one of the keystones is reducing soil disturbance and empowering nature to do its job – keeping it fields alive. Regenerative farming is not just a cool trend, it is a fascinating journey allowing farmers to benefit themselves while diminishing erosion and enriching their prime tool – soil.

Rapeseed drill
RSL

50 cm row spacing for rapeseed drilling provides optimal conditions for quick sprouting, strong plants, wintering, great yield, low capital investment, cost efficiency and many more. It is a proven system that hardly can anyone compete with. A specialized drill designed only for rapeseed seeding.

Universal Drill
RS

Having a drill for all type crop seeding is very beneficial especially when it works in a strip till concept. Seed bands of 15 cm every 33-36 cm distance work exceptionally well for grains. Adding mineral and liquid fertilizers on the same go combined with machine’s high quality makes the product stand out.
