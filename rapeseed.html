RSL rapeseed strip till drilling

Growing rapeseed/oilseed is one of the most profitable crops in farming. Though, profitability is not defined by high prices of rapeseed in the market. As in every business profit comes from positive subtraction of total turnover and costs, including fields preparation, seeding costs, fertilizers, spraying, harvesting, drying, etc. Strip tilling highly reduce costs in general. Fields preparation in conventional farming is a very expensive addition to overall costs. Rapeseed strip till drilling omits many unnecessary operations. Farming this way allows you to increase profitability, as well your farm will diminish erosion and help to improve fertility of your soils.

In rapeseed strip till drilling, we are not pursuing best yields, but seek top returns. For this reason, Laumetris has created a specialized strip till seeder only for rapeseed drilling. A tool designed for one purpose and one purpose only. RSL is not just a conceptional strip till drill, it is the machine which every attribute, every working part is developed to create the best conditions for rapeseed growing.

Rapeseed root is thick and goes deep into soil, for this reason RSL has special tines with possibility to till up to 30 cm of depth. All tines are equipped with wear points having carbide tiles – and it is not an extra option, it is standard. It is not cherry picking, Laumetris heavier class RSL drills are equipped with superior wear points specifically designed and manufactured in the UK at Chapman’s having 15 carbide tiles. Lighter class RSL-L models have thinner Hardox steel tines and wear points with two bigger carbide tiles.

Stainless steel tank for fertilizers assures durability and automatic rate control provides stress-free usage of the machine. Control of liquid or mineral fertilization systems is done through the APV control box or ISOBUS. Calibration is quick and simple.

Distance between strips in RSL models are 50 or 60 cm. It might seem big but rapeseed is not a standard cash crop. By increasing distance between strips, we reduce competition between plants. Rapeseed, having more nutrition from ground and space, grows additional side branches, which allows to acquire more sun and inevitably become stronger. Such plant has much more yield potential compared to a conventionally seeded rapeseed plant. Roots are considerably thicker concentrating more reserves, pests like Ceutorhynchus pallidactylus larvae do not kill the plant. Big strip distance allows to dramatically reduce seeding rate, target is 25-30 plants per square meter or 1,5-2,2 kg/ha. Again, this reduces costs of seed per season almost by double (considering 50 plants per square meter in conventional till) and harvesting yields are the same. Sometimes less is more.

Rapeseed strip till drilling combines the best attributes of both no tillage and conventional tillage technologies

1) Stubble is the best protection against erosion, as well topsoil protection highly reduces moisture evaporation, prevents weeds growing. Stubble also helps to linger snow during winter – better probability to withstand cold.

2) Soil is being tilled only at the seeding zone and narrow strip beneath it – the rest is untilled keeping soil structure unbroken. Low disturbance keeps soil aggregates intact, which benefits better water infiltration, maintains organic matter, keeps mycorrhizal fungal networks.

3) Earthworms are a sign of a healthy soil. It helps to convert old stubble to nutrients for freshly sprouted plants and improves soil fertility.

4) Laumetris RS and RSL drills prepare seedbeds in the best conditions for rapid germination. One of the key factors is bringing moisture to topsoil from beneath.

5) Mineral fertilizers are distributed throughout 5-15 cm of depth. 2/3 clients using Laumetris strip till drills combined together with mineral (BF) and liquid (LF) fertilization systems. Spraying of start-up liquid fertilizers adds up to rapid sprouting, mineral fertilizers are being consumed at a later stage.

6) Strip till technology enables farmers to reduce seeding rate as plants receive more sun, have less competition, better conditions to sprout, grow, have more side branches, stronger stems, are healthier and harvest results at the end stay the same or even increased. Comparing how many tillage processes are dropped, fuel and time saved – strip till technology highly increases profitability of the farm. CO2 emissions are reduced as a consequence of using strip till.
